If you read nothing else

Every supplement sells you an outcome — sleep, focus, longevity. Signal sells the layer underneath all of them: cellular energy, the foundation your whole stack is standing on. One product, a daily chew, seven branded actives named at real doses. We don't promise a result; we name the dose and show our work — and we build the one thing that makes any foundation matter: a product you'll actually keep taking.

We sell the floor, not the outcome.

This didn't start as a marketing angle. It started with watching the people who taught us what health really costs — and deciding to build the one layer the whole industry skips past on its way to selling a result. Foundation first, because everything else is standing on it.

The documents below build that idea out — from what the brand believes, to who it talks to, to what's in the box, to how the money works, to how it launches. Read top to bottom for the full picture, or jump by colour: brand, strategy, product & money, the pitch.

Why this is worth building

Every brand in this category is racing to sell the next outcome — the better sleep, the sharper focus, the longer life. Signal is building the floor underneath all of them — the layer that stays true no matter which outcome is in fashion this year. That isn't another product fighting for shelf space. It's a category nobody owns yet, and the door is open.

Foundation first. Everything stacks.
