================================================================
SIGNAL — "THE FORCE OF NATURE" BRAND FILM
AI VIDEO GENERATION PROMPT PACK  ·  v1 · 2026-06-14
60-second category-mood film · one fire, many lifeforms
================================================================

HOW TO USE THIS FILE
--------------------
AI video tools (Runway Gen-4, Kling, Veo, Sora, Pika) generate the
ORGANIC CLIPS below — one prompt per generation, ~5–8s each. They will
NOT do three things, so you do these in an editor afterward:
  1. The MATCH-CUTS between shots (assembled, not generated).
  2. The exact WORDMARK   -> overlay  assets/signal_wordmark_on_dark.png
  3. The exact SIGNAL SOUND -> lay in assets/signal_sound.wav
Workflow: generate clips -> pick best takes -> assemble + color-grade
-> overlay wordmark -> lay in sound -> export. (See ASSEMBLY at bottom.)

THE ONE RULE (do not break)
---------------------------
The product NEVER appears as the cause of a natural event. No sachet by
an egg, no chew dissolving into a bloom. Nature carries the awe; the
product, if shown at all, comes only AFTER the wordmark, separately.


================================================================
GLOBAL STYLE BIBLE  —  prepend (or set as style ref) on EVERY shot
================================================================
Cinematic macro nature-documentary look. Extreme close-up, shallow
depth of field, anamorphic, photoreal, fine 35mm film grain. Palette:
deep espresso-brown shadows, soft bone-cream highlights, and a single
warm OXBLOOD-RED internal glow (#AD4236) that pulses from inside living
tissue like bioluminescent energy. Low-key dramatic lighting,
volumetric light, deep negative space. Slow, deliberate motion. The
red-amber internal pulse is the through-line of the whole film — it
must appear, subtly, in every shot.

GLOBAL NEGATIVE PROMPT  —  append on every shot
-----------------------------------------------
text, captions, subtitles, watermark, logo, brand name, UI, distorted
anatomy, extra fingers, extra limbs, mutated hands, cartoon, anime,
plastic CGI look, oversaturated, neon, busy background, fast cutting,
shaky cam


================================================================
THE SHOTS  (in order — each row is one generation)
================================================================

--- SHOT 1 · THE DARK (the floor) · ~8s ---
Extreme macro, underground cross-section of dark wet soil. A single
seed casing slowly splits open; the first pale root tip pushes against
grit. A faint warm amber-red glow pulses from deep inside the seed.
Camera: very slow push-in toward the seed's interior. Near-black frame,
single low key light, drifting dust motes. Slow motion.

--- SHOT 2 · THE UNBORN HEART (effort before the crack) · ~7s ---
Extreme macro, a translucent egg backlit in darkness. A glowing network
of fine blood vessels is visible through the shell; a tiny embryonic
heart beats rhythmically inside the INTACT shell. The amber-red glow
pulses in time with the heartbeat. Camera: slow drift across the
vascular network. Egg lit from within, dark surround. Slow motion.
(Do NOT show hatching or a cracked shell.)

--- SHOT 3 · THE NIGHT SHIFT (labor, not glory) · ~7s ---
Extreme macro, a spider spinning silk in pre-dawn darkness — precise,
repetitive labor on a half-built web. A single glistening silk strand
under tension catches a faint amber rim light; a subtle red glow inside
the spider's body. Camera: locked-off, then a slow track along the
strand. Low-key, single rim light, deep shadow. Slow motion.
(No dew, no sunrise, no finished web.)

--- SHOT 4A · ROOT THROUGH GRIT (widening) · ~5s ---
Extreme macro timelapse, a plant root forcing its way through dense
gritty soil, shouldering aside small stones, relentless growth. A faint
amber-red energy glow runs and pulses along the root. Camera: slow
follow of the advancing tip. Dramatic dark side-light.

--- SHOT 4B · AGAINST THE CURRENT (widening) · ~5s ---
Underwater, a powerful salmon swimming hard against a fast river
current, flank muscles flexing, water turbulence streaming past. A
faint internal amber-red glow under the skin suggests muscular energy.
Cold blue water against the warm internal glow. Camera: tracking
alongside the fish. Slow motion. (No people.)

--- SHOT 5 · THE TURN (the whole point) · ~8s ---
Cinematic close-up: a weathered older human hand grips a wooden stair
railing, knuckles tightening with effort. Then a wider, tender shot of
an adult carrying a small child up a staircase — an ordinary domestic
moment. A faint warm amber-red glow pulses beneath the skin of the
straining muscles. Warm low light, shallow depth of field, faces soft
and not the focus. Slow motion.
(NOTE: AI struggles with hands — generate several takes, pick clean
fingers, or frame tighter to avoid full-hand detail.)

--- SHOT 6 · THE PAYOFFS, UNDERSOLD (deliberately not the climax) · ~6s ---
Fast, understated montage of brief flashes, warm natural light: a
flower opening in timelapse; an eggshell cracking; a spider web
catching the first gold light; a person reaching the top of a
staircase. Each flash is brief and quiet, not triumphant. Photoreal.

--- SHOT 7 · THE RESET (never arrives, never stops) · ~6s ---
Extreme macro, return to dark soil. A NEW single seed rests in black
earth; a faint amber-red glow begins to pulse inside it — the cycle
starting again. Very slow push-in. Near-black, volumetric dust. Slow
motion. (A variant of Shot 1; can reuse/regrade Shot 1's best take.)

--- SHOT 8 · THE CLOSE · NOT AI-GENERATED — built in the editor ---
Black screen. Overlay the wordmark (assets/signal_wordmark_on_dark.png)
as TWO copies, offset horizontally so it reads doubled and slightly out
of alignment. Over ~1.5s, animate the two copies converging into one
perfectly aligned mark. Lay in assets/signal_sound.wav so the two tones
resolve to one pitch EXACTLY as the marks lock — the logo becomes clear
as the sound fades. Then add the line, small, beneath: send the right
signal.


================================================================
ASSEMBLY (in your editor — CapCut / Premiere / DaVinci / Runway)
================================================================
TIMING (60s master):
  0:00 Shot1  0:08 Shot2  0:16 Shot3  0:24 Shot4A+4B  0:32 Shot5 (hold,
  the peak — let it breathe)  0:42 Shot6  0:50 Shot7  0:56 Shot8 close.

MATCH-CUTS: cut on the PULSE, not the subject. At each transition,
align the amber-red glow of the outgoing shot with the glow of the
incoming shot; use a quick dissolve or whip on the glow so it reads as
ONE continuous fire moving through different life-forms.

COLOR: AI clips won't match out of the box. Grade them all to one look
— espresso shadows, bone highlights, the single oxblood-red glow. If a
clip lost the internal glow, add it back as a subtle masked glow comp.

AUDIO:
  - Bed: a low heartbeat/pulse ambient track runs throughout (temp or
    licensed). It rises to Shot 5, then strips back to a single pulse
    by 0:56.
  - Sting: at ~0:56 lay assets/signal_sound.wav over black for the
    wordmark converge. ONE sonic event, at the close only — never
    mid-roll. Loudness-normalize the final mix to -14 LUFS.

CUTDOWNS: :30 / :15 / :06 reuse the same spine. The :06 is just Shot 5
(the turn) + Shot 8 (the sonic close). Also export 9:16 vertical
reframes for paid/organic.
