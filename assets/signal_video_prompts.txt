THE IDEA (so every choice makes sense)
--------------------------------------
Every supplement sells the MOMENT — focus, power, recovery, glow. This
film shows the ENERGY COILING toward that moment, creature after
creature, and never lets the release happen. The withheld payoff IS the
brand: "we name the dose, not the outcome." The film builds a charge it
refuses to spend, so the charge goes into the VIEWER. Goal, in one word:
PRIMED. An athlete should watch it and feel it in their legs.

HOW TO USE THIS FILE
--------------------
AI generates the COIL CLIPS below (one prompt per generation, ~5–8s).
The film lives in the EDIT RHYTHM and the SOUND — neither is generated.
In an editor you then:
  1. Cut to an ACCELERATING rhythm (long holds → sub-second).
  2. Lay signal_tension_bed.wav under the whole film (rises, never resolves).
  3. DENY the release: cut the bed dead into ~2s near-silence.
  4. Fire signal_sound.wav (the gun) + snap the wordmark overlay.
Wordmark + sound are SUPPLIED FILES — never ask the AI to draw/guess them.

THE ONE RULE (do not break)
---------------------------
Never show the action itself — no strike, no leap, no flight, no lift.
Every shot stops at the "moment before." And the product never appears
as the cause of anything; nature carries it, the product is separate.

GLOBAL STYLE BIBLE  —  prepend (or set as style ref) on EVERY shot
================================================================
Cinematic macro nature-documentary look. Extreme close-up, shallow
depth of field, anamorphic, photoreal, fine 35mm film grain. Palette:
deep espresso-brown shadows, soft bone-cream highlights, a single warm
OXBLOOD-RED internal glow (#AD4236) pulsing from inside living tissue
like bioluminescent energy. Low-key dramatic lighting, volumetric light,
deep negative space. EVERY SHOT IS A "MOMENT BEFORE" — held tension,
coiled potential energy, on the verge of an explosive action that never
comes. Slow, taut, charged motion (not relaxed). The red-amber pulse is
the through-line — subtle, in every shot.

GLOBAL NEGATIVE PROMPT  —  append on every shot
-----------------------------------------------
the action itself, strike, leap, jump, flight, release, the catch,
relaxed or calm posture, text, captions, watermark, logo, brand name,
distorted anatomy, extra fingers, mutated hands, cartoon, anime, plastic
CGI look, oversaturated, neon, shaky cam


================================================================
THE COIL SHOTS  (in order — each is one generation)
Cut them so the HOLDS SHORTEN as the film climbs.
================================================================

--- SHOT 1 · THE STILLNESS (focus) · ~8s · longest hold ---
Extreme close-up of a great horned owl (or a leopard) in absolute
stillness, pupils dilated huge, eyes locked on unseen prey, head utterly
motionless, every muscle coiled beneath the surface. A faint warm
amber-red glow pulses under the skin/feathers. Camera: slow, almost
imperceptible push-in. Near-black surround, single low key light,
volumetric haze. Slow. Held tension — the instant before a strike.

--- SHOT 2 · THE LOAD (power) · ~6s ---
Extreme macro of a powerful animal's hindquarter and tendons drawing
tight, muscle bunching and loading under the skin, weight shifting back
— a coiled spring compressing. A warm amber-red glow pools where the
force gathers. Camera: slow track across the loading muscle. Dramatic
dark side-light. Slow, taut motion. The moment before a leap.

--- SHOT 3 · THE COMPRESSION (drive) · ~5s ---
A salmon bending its whole body like a drawn bow against a powerful
waterfall current, coiling to launch upward, water tearing past, muscles
straining. Faint amber-red glow under the skin. Camera: tracking
alongside, slow motion. Cold blue water against the warm internal glow.

--- SHOT 4 · THE EDGE (launch) · ~5s ---
A vast dense flock of starlings massed at the edge of a cliff at dusk,
thousands of wings tensing and shuffling, the whole collective leaning
toward the drop, about to launch — held on the brink. A faint amber glow
ripples through the mass. Camera: slow drift across the swarm. Moody low
light.

--- SHOT 5 · THE GATHER (the chase) · ~5s ---
Extreme close-up of a cheetah's final crouch before a sprint — shoulders
dropping low, haunches winding tight, gaze locked, the instant before
explosion. (Alt: a snake drawn fully into its strike-coil.) A warm
amber-red glow under the skin where the power gathers. Camera: slow
push-in. Dark, dramatic light. Slow motion.

--- SHOT 6 · THE HUMAN (the bridge) · ~6s · generate 2–3 takes ---
Cinematic close-ups, held breath, of a human at the threshold of action:
a diver curled tight on the platform edge; knuckles whitening on a
loaded barbell; a performer in the dark wings of a stage facing the
light, chest filling with a held inhale. A faint warm amber-red glow
pulses beneath the straining skin. Warm low light, shallow DOF, faces
soft and not the focus. Taut stillness.
(NOTE: AI struggles with hands — generate several takes, pick clean
fingers, or frame tighter. Keep ONE non-athletic human so it's not a
pre-workout ad.)

NOT GENERATED — built in the editor
================================================================

--- THE STACK (0:47–0:55) ---
Re-use the best frames of Shots 1–6 at maximum compression, intercut
sub-second and accelerating, building to a single frame that PROMISES
the release.

--- THE DENIAL (0:55–0:57) ---
Hard cut to BLACK. ~2s of near-silence (cut the tension bed dead). The
release everything built toward — gone.

--- THE GUN (0:57–1:00) ---
On black, overlay the wordmark (signal_wordmark_on_dark.png) as TWO
copies, offset horizontally and dim. On the gun's transient they SNAP
into one perfectly-aligned mark (a hard lock, NOT a slow drift). Lay in
signal_sound.wav so the two tones snap to unison exactly as the marks
lock. Add the line beneath, small: send the right signal. Cut to black.

ASSEMBLY (in your editor)
================================================================
TIMING (60s):
  0:00 Stillness(~10s) · 0:10 Load(~8) · 0:18 Compression(~7) ·
  0:25 Edge(~7) · 0:32 Gather(~5) · 0:39 Human(~3, fast intercut) ·
  0:47 Stack(<1s cuts) · 0:55 DENY(~2s black) · 0:57 GUN(~3s).

RHYTHM IS THE FILM: cut on the amber-red PULSE (not the subject), and
make each hold SHORTER than the last. That acceleration is the priming.
A flat cut rhythm kills it no matter how good the clips are.

COLOR: grade all clips to one look (espresso shadows, bone highlights,
single oxblood glow). Re-add the internal glow as a masked comp where a
clip lost it — it's the connective tissue across every cut.

AUDIO:
  - signal_tension_bed.wav under the whole film: two tones held ~10 Hz
    apart (beating, never resolving), rising, over an accelerating
    heartbeat. It must feel like it NEEDS to resolve and won't.
  - At the denial, CUT THE BED DEAD into ~2s near-silence.
  - Then fire signal_sound.wav (the gun) — the snap to unison — over the
    wordmark lock. This is the only release in the film.
  - Final mix to -14 LUFS.

CUTDOWNS: :30 / :15 / :06 reuse the coil. The :06 = one coil + the
denial + the gun. Export 9:16 vertical reframes for paid/organic.
