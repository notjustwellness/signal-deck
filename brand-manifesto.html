II — The Mechanism

Your cells run on Mg‑ATP.

ATP is the energy every cell in your body uses to do its job — repair DNA, build collagen, fire a thought, hold water in tissue. But ATP only works in its magnesium-bound form. Free ATP is biologically inert. Approximately 90% of functional cellular ATP exists as Mg-ATP.

Creatine is the fastest way your cells regenerate ATP once it's spent. And creatine kinase — the enzyme that does the regenerating — requires magnesium to function. Mg²⁺ is also a cofactor for 600+ enzymatic reactions, including the rate-limiting mitochondrial dehydrogenases.
