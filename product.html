·The six — named, dosed, sourced

Each active is dosed to its clinically evidenced level — the formulation ethos is minimum effective dose, maximum effect: enough to do real work, nothing wasted. The mechanisms below describe what each ingredient does inside the cell, in plain terms — they are not promises of how you'll feel, and any consumer-facing wording is routed through Zone Halo before it touches a label.

Magnesium bisglycinate

~200 mg elemental
Albion TRAACS® · Balchem · (~1.5 g raw, chelated for absorption)

The mineral the cell's energy currency literally runs on — roughly 9 of every 10 ATP molecules carry magnesium; the rest sit idle. The bisglycinate (glycine-chelated) form is chosen for gentleness and uptake over cheap oxide.

Cost: dose known · quote pending

Kaneka QH® ubiquinol

100 mg
Kaneka (Japan) · the original ubiquinol

The active, body-ready form of CoQ10 — a core component of the cell's energy machinery. The single most expensive line in the formula, and the one whose grade sets the width of our cost range.

Real supplier quote in hand

BioPQQ®

~20 mg
Mitsubishi Gas Chemical

Pyrroloquinoline quinone — associated in the literature with the cell building new mitochondria (the energy plants themselves), rather than just feeding the ones you have.

Cost: dose known · quote pending

MitoPrime® ergothioneine

10 mg
Nutrition 21 · "the longevity vitamin"

A cellular antioxidant the body actively transports into cells and concentrates where energy is made — protecting the machinery from its own exhaust.

Real anchor · $980/kg

Puremidine® spermidine

~3 mg (≥98%)
NNB Nutrition

Supports autophagy — the cell's housekeeping process of clearing out worn, damaged parts. Chosen as a purified ~3 mg active in place of an 800 mg wheat-germ extract: same job, no gram-scale bulk.

Cost: dose known · quote pending

PurestCreatine

5 g
NNB Nutrition · creatine monohydrate

The cell's rapid-recharge buffer for ATP — the most-studied energy substrate there is. The one gram-scale active in the box; its 5 g dose is the formulation's biggest format challenge (see fences).

Real anchor · $11/kg
