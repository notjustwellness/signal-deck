01 — The marks

Two pieces of artwork. Nothing else.

Every template on this page is built from exactly two assets: the traced wordmark and the /s/ monogram. Never re-typeset either one — always place the artwork.

Wordmark/ sɪgnɑːl /
Monogram — primaryspaced /s/
Monogram — smalltight /s/
Clear space= height of the "s"

The one rule: red lives in the brackets. The slashes are always Signal Red #AD4236; the letterforms shift with the ground (bone on dark, espresso on light). One red per surface — never two.

06 — Social

The social voice

Top-of-funnel formats that read native, not like ads — but every one anchors mono top-left / wordmark foot so recognition holds. Vary the words; never the frame. That repetition is the brand.
